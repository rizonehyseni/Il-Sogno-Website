About Us

At Il Sogno, we believe that dining is more than just a meal, it’s an experience that nourishes the soul. Inspired by the timeless elegance of Italian tradition, our restaurant was founded to bring a genuine slice of Italy to Amalfi. Every detail at Il Sogno reflects our commitment to authenticity and refinement: from the warm, inviting atmosphere to the thoughtfully crafted dishes that honor recipes passed down through generations. We source only the freshest, finest ingredients to create flavors that are both familiar and unforgettable. Here, you are not just a guest, you are part of our extended family. Whether sharing laughter over a candlelit dinner or savoring a quiet moment with a glass of carefully selected wine, Il Sogno invites you to slow down, savor the present, and live the dream of true Italian hospitality.

Our Menu

A celebration of coastal Italian cuisine, our menu is inspired by the soul of Amalfi and the rhythm of late-night jazz. Crafted with seasonal ingredients, each dish blends tradition and creativity — inviting you to slow down, savor the moment, and experience la dolce vita.
