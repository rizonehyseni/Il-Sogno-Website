About Us

Our Roots

Whispered by the sea breeze of Amalfi, our story began in the hush between waves and moonlight. Each recipe carries the salt of the coast and the warmth of an Italian soul — ancient, enduring, alive.

Our Philosophy

We move to the rhythm of simplicity and soul. Every flavor a note, every dish a melody. Here, food and feeling entwine — a quiet symphony of passion, patience, and devotion.

Our Ambience

Shadows dance beneath a velvet glow. The murmur of jazz drifts through the air, mingling with the scent of citrus and wine. It is night in Amalfi — intimate, mysterious, eternal.

Our Menu

A celebration of coastal Italian cuisine, our menu is inspired by the soul of Amalfi and the rhythm of late-night jazz. Crafted with seasonal ingredients, each dish blends tradition and creativity — inviting you to slow down, savor the moment, and experience la dolce vita.

Music Nights

Live Music, Candlelight, and Atmosphere Every Saturday evening, we dim the lights and let the music set the tone. Whether it's a soft jazz duo or an acoustic set under the stars, our Music Nights create the perfect backdrop for connection.
